How Do I Keep My Camo Cover Looking Spick and Span?


Keeping your camo phone cover clean isn't rocket science, but a bit of TLC will keep it looking top-notch for longer.

Here's how to do it:

First things first, take the cover off your phone.

We don't want any water sneaking in where it shouldn't!

Grab a soft cloth - one that won't leave bits of fluff all over your cover.

Give it a gentle wipe over to get rid of any obvious muck.

If it's looking a bit grimy, mix up some warm water with a squirt of washing-up liquid.

Dip your cloth in (don't soak it, mind) and give your cover a good once-over.

Got a silicone or rubber cover? You can use an old toothbrush to get into all the nooks and crannies.

Just be gentle, yeah?

If you've got a proper stubborn stain, try making a paste with some baking soda and water.

Dab it on, leave it for a few minutes, then give it a scrub.

Works a treat!

Once you're done, use a clean, damp cloth to wipe off any soap, then pop your cover on a towel to dry.

Don't stick it on a radiator or in direct sunlight - that could make the colours fade or warp the shape.
